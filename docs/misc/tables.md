# Laue groups

Patterson symmetry is the Laue class (or group) plus allowed centring.

##2D
Laue class | Patterson symmetry  | plane groups  | count
-----------| ------------------- |---------------|-------
**2**   | p2     | p1,p2                             | 2
**2mm** | p2mm   | p1m1,p1g1,p2mm,p2gg,p2gm          | 5
        | c2mm   | c1m1,c2mm                         | 2
**4**   | p4     | p4                                | 1
**4mm** | p4mm   | p4mm,p4gm                         | 2
**6**   | p6     | p3,p6                             | 2
**6mm** | p6mm   | p3m1,p31m,p6mm                    | 3
